Sickness Allowance form

Matilda, our records indicate you are eligible for a sickness allowance. You may need to complete some extra forms. This will help us to accurately assess your claim, based on your and your partner's individual circumstances.

Separate forms are required if you:

  • Are self-employed or involved in a business
  • Have involvement in a private company
  • Have involvement in a private trust
  • Currently pay rent where you live
  • Are a resident in Australia or another country
  • Have separated from your partner since you last claimed a payment or service
  • Have recently left your employment
Step 1: Review your details

Please review your personal details and update them if required.

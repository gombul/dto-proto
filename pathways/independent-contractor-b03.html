Independent contractors decision tool

This simple independent contractors decision tool should be used as a guide only and doesn't have any legal force or effect. It provides general advice on whether you are considered an independent contractor by the following governing bodies.

You are considered an independent contractor by:
ATO Fairwork Industry State Courts
Do you own the things you need to do your work?
Do you plan to sub-contract work to someone else?
Do you want to control how and when you work?

Use the full independent contractor decision tools on agency websites for clearer understanding of whether or not you are considered an independent contractor.

Whether you're a worker or someone who pays a worker, the independent contractors decision tool can help you understand what your working relationship is.

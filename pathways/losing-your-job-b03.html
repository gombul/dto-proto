Government money for job seekers

The Australian Government can give money to you if you are looking for work, completing approved studies or have a disability and are looking for work.

On this page:

Income support while you look for work

If you pay or receive child support you should make sure we know about your change of situation. Changes to your situation may affect the amount of child support you pay or receive. You can update your child support arrangement by using Child Support online accounts. Read more about changes that affect your child support.

Newstart allowance

Newstart Allowance provides financial help if you are looking for work or taking part in activities to find a job.

 After signing into your account, you will be directed to the Department of Human Services to apply.

Youth Allowance

Youth Allowance supports young people 16 to 21 years of age, who are looking for full time work or undertaking approved activities.

 After signing into your account, you will be directed to the Department of Human Services to apply.

Participation Activity Record

Use the Participation Activity Record form to keep a record of your jobsearch efforts or activities.

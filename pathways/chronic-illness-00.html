Prototype: Diagnosed with chronic illness

Matilda

  • I am a 50 year old woman
  • I live alone, and I have just been diagnosed with Type 2 diabetes
  • I want to know:
    • If support is available for medication and lifestyle changes
    • My eligibility for a health care card and health services
    • How does the disease progress and associated conditions

Assumptions

  • This person already has an online account and is a known customer to government. No registration etc required.
  • This person is starting at a search engine and goes staight to a third party in all scenarios.
  • Partially assisted transaction/Face-to-face service required.
  • A generic brand is being used to remain neutral to any branding discussion or decision that is happening in parallel.

- Generic content consumed on Diabetes Australia (DA) with a link to individual.gov.au for detailed content, login and transaction
- A variant of Scenario A with additional links to individual.gov.au for detailed content, login and transaction
- A variant of Scenario B with direct links to agency websites for detailed content, login and transaction

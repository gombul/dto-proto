Information layer prototype index

The purpose of these prototypes is to visualise high level design options for the information layer. This is a way to make abstract concepts more real and understandable, and also to prompt discussion and shake out issues and alternative ideas. These are first drafts and will be continually iterated as more people provide feedback. Detailed content and functionality is for illustrative purposes and may not be accurate or comprehensive.

Prototypes:

  1. Losing your job
  2. Expanding your business
  3. Set up as an Independent Contractor
  4. Diagnosed with chronic illness
  5. Study in Australia

Navigating through the scenarios

Each screen has a coloured bar at the top. This contains basic instruction for what to click in order to progress through the scenario.
